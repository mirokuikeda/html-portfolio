The Best Movies According to Sohom

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Castle in the Sky

I love adventure movies and this was one of the first ones I watched. It's a Studio Ghibli production. All Studio Ghibli productions by default are my favorites.

Princess Mononoke

Being pro environment and pro earth, I absolutely adore environment-themed movies. And this one has an emotional story to tell.

Nausica Valley of the Wind

Another Studio Ghibli masterpiece. Another environment-themed movie. This time dealing with a post-apocalyptic dystopia. Amazing watch.
